icossymetry

go back

This whole thing started when i wanted to recreate the floor pattern in this iconic maths video, Not Knot by the Geometry Center. I knew i couldn't really recreate it bc it was a hyperbolic tiling, but i still tried to do something similar.

The idea was to make the icosahedron's symmetry group out of circle beziers, give them thickness then project their shadow onto a plane below it all.
