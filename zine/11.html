Plants. They are everywhere. But not many people really think much about them or wonder how they work, what they are and why they matter. Which is a shame! Plants can be beautiful, astonishing, and indeed, very important for basically every living being on this planet.

Your cells need oxygen, brought from the air into your lungs, then blood, so it can enter a series of reactions forming ATP, which is used to synthesize proteins used by your body to... keep existing. And that oxygen will most likely come from plants. But what are they?
