Plants can be of two sorts: Sporophytes or Gametophytes. They are different in many ways: Sporophytes have 2 sets of chromosomes, that is they have 2 copies of the same chromosomes. Gametophytes, however, only have one copy of every chromosome. Another difference is how they reproduce. You can tell by their names!

Sporophytes reproduce asexually via spores, while Gametophytes reproduce sexually with female and male gametes. Now, you may ask, why does that matter?
