Ok, this is getting a bit long, so i'll try to be short: Let's say we have a mango tree. The tree is a Sporophyte with 2 sets of chromosomes, which produces spores that, through metagenesis and meiosis, turn into Gametophytes that have just one set of chromosomes; they are the female embryo sac and the male... pollen! The "son" of a tree is actually the pollen in the flowers, not another tree!
