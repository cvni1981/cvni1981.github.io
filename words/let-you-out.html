let you out

One might feel trapped.
Within themselves.
Their rules hold them.

But, at any time, an instinct may occur.
At once.

The young artist,
when they discover it,
and its freedom.
They feel free.
No borders, no limits!
An infinite plane.
Its sudden nature might feel strange at first.
For in the paper, there are no rules.
You can be.
But it becomes sublime.
As with all times, it takes time.
Let you out.
