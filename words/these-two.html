these two

You can't ignore their power,
these 2...
first thing one sees, they tell signs
words can't combine
a looking glass, a window pane
let out what it can't contain
and connect in ways subliminal
on show for all to see. At no cost.
Deeper than the sea
every pair is unique,
these 2 act by instinct.
They show all, they don't wait.
