:owe:

  • orwellian, authoritative tone; jokingly implies some kind of abuse of authority
    "do x" "no :owe:"
  • imperative marker
    "do the survey :owe:"
  • mods are doing something, good or bad (contextual)
  • combination of /j and /lh; general joke marker

:what:

  • /s
  • disbelief; confusion; objectionable action
    "i can't believe this"
  • you feel bad about something but there's not much you can do about it
    "well. that sucks"
  • implies something makes the user uncomfortable/in pain

:deepfriedwhat:

  • same as :what: or what but more intense; even more objectionable opinion
    "I CAN'T BELIEVE THIS"

:iceface:

  • an extremely more intense version of :what:; something so bizzare you are speechless/stunned; :what: with a tinge of pain
  • a crime against God

:hog:

  • pog; celebratory (both genuine and sarcastic)
    "holy shit!!!"

:mercuryiioxide:

  • the same as :hog:
  • more obviously sarcastic version of :hog:
  • a response to literally any statement
    "not a fan of homosexuals" ":mercuryiioxide: damn bro that's crazy"

:gatosurprise:

  • fear; shock; devastation
    "wh"
  • mix of :hog: and :what:
  • more genuine version of :waltuh:

:florshed:

  • embarrasment, possibly due to a compliment
  • response to something surprising and/or jokingly sexual
  • same as/exageration of/full replacement of :flushed:

:floshed:

  • synonym of :florshed:

:emoji:

  • similar to :what: but in reaction to something dumb
  • speechless in a neutral way
  • just the face
